Your Reality

Verse 1:

Every day, I imagine a future where I can be with you In my hand is a pen that will write a poem of me and you. The ink flows down into a dark puddle Just move your hand, the way into his heart. But in this world of infinite choices What will it take just to find that special day? What will it take just to find that special day?
